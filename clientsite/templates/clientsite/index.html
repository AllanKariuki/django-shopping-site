Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have Warranty?

Yes. We provide 12 months warranty for new printers and copiers, and 6 months warranty for refurbished printers and copiers.

How long do you take to deliver?

You will love our delivery services. We dispatch immediately you place your order!
Order early to have your products delivered the same day.

What is the difference Between New and Refurbished?

New products are just that: new and boxed.
Refurbished machines are used products that have been reconditioned, sometimes overhauled.

Do you have technicians?

We have the best technical team always ready on call.

So what happens after I place my order?

You will receive an email from us confirming your order. We'll then call you when dispatching, and send you the package details.

Can I pay on delivery

Yes. For Photocopiers

Toners and spare parts need to be paid for before dispatch.
